I attended the Apple annual conference WWDC 2016 as a scholarship winner. (Check out how I did in this previous article)

I spent a wonderful week in San Francisco learning, networking and meeting my friends and my old Apple colleagues.

Bellow, I will describe the main things that happened this week, my personal WWDC 2016 experience and conclude with some useful tips if you are planning to attend WWDC in the coming years.

Sunday: Badge pick-up or Scholarship Orientation Day

For scholarship winners, Sunday represents the Scholarship Orientation Day where they meet other winners, hear insights from Apple Engineers and get advice on how to get most out of WWDC. Check out this article describing more in details this organized event by Apple.

Before heading off to Mission Bay Conference, where the event was held, my curiosity led me to go to Bill Graham Civic Auditorium, where the other attendees were picking up their badges and WWDC jacket.
