Hi. I'm Sanaa.

I'm a dynamic macOS developer, with great attention to detail and a passion for generating compelling user experiences. I'm currently working at Dashlane. I have a strong interest in the latest technologies and focuses on getting the Dashlane Mac application to the next level.

I enjoy traveling internationally and when I'm not working, you are likely to find me at the gym or spending time with friends and family!
